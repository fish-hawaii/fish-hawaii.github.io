## Area Types Explained

| Abbreviation | Full Name | Description |
|---|---|---|
| MLCD | Marine Life Conservation District | Highly protected areas; fishing is generally prohibited or severely restricted |
| FMA | Fisheries Management Area | Areas with specific gear, species, or method restrictions |
| CBSFA | Community-Based Subsistence Fishing Area | Areas managed with local community input; subsistence-focused rules |
| FRA | Fish Replenishment Area | Areas within the West Hawai'i Regional FMA with additional protections |
| NRA | Netting Restricted Area | Areas within West Hawai'i where lay net fishing is prohibited |
| PFA | Public Fishing Area | Freshwater or shoreline areas designated for public recreational fishing |
| NAR | Natural Area Reserve | State nature reserves; fishing generally prohibited |
